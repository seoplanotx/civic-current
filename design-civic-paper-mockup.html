What changed, and why

Paper background, cream panels. Replaces bg-slate-950 + glow orbs + glassmorphism. Removes the v0-dashboard signature instantly while keeping shape language identical, so engineering effort stays low.
Hero / secondary split. Three big stats (Treasury, Power, Approval) get serif numerals and bars. Population, Jobs, Environment, Reliability, Influence demote to a quieter row. Player gets a glanceable answer to "am I OK right now?" without parsing eight identical chips.
Sentence case everywhere. No more UPPERCASE TRACKING-WIDEST labels, no all-caps buttons. "Build", "Demolish · $50", "View details" — labels that read like a tool, not a sci-fi briefing.
One color = one meaning. Green (good), blue (info / civic), gold (warning / load), red (cost / danger). No indigo/teal/cyan/rose/violet/amber/emerald cocktail. Tags reuse the same four colors as the ledger numbers.
Serif for numerals and titles only. Fraunces (or any humanist serif you prefer) on stat numbers, panel titles, and building names — it carries the "civic ledger" feel. Inter handles everything else. Resists the "AI cyber font stack" feel without going twee.
Animation removed. Nothing pulses, bounces, or breathes in the resting state. Reserve motion for delta flashes (a 600ms ease-out fade on a value when it changes this turn).
